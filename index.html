Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 11, 2026

Thank you for using AI Detection and Counting of Cotton Whitefly developed by ICAR-Central Institute for Cotton Research (ICAR-CICR).

This Privacy Policy explains how the application handles user information and data.

1. Overview

The application is designed to detect and count insects from images captured or uploaded by users. The app operates completely offline on the user’s device and does not require internet connectivity.

2. Information Collection

  • Does not collect personal information.
  • Does not require user registration or login.
  • Does not track users.
  • Does not store user data on external servers.
  • Does not share user information with third parties.
  • Does not use analytics or advertising services.

3. Permissions

Camera Permission

Used to capture images for insect detection and counting.

Storage Permission

Used to select images, save CSV reports locally, and share CSV files manually.

4. Offline Functionality

The application functions entirely offline. No user images, CSV files, or detection results are uploaded to the internet.

5. Data Storage

All generated data is stored locally on the user's device.

6. Data Sharing

The application does not automatically share any data. Users may manually share CSV files at their own discretion.

7. Children's Privacy

This application does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Security

Since the app works fully offline, user data remains on the local device environment.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in future versions.
